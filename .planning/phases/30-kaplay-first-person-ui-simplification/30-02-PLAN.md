## Objective

## Root Cause

Kaplay's `k.text()` supports styled text: `[red]...[/red]`, `[wobble]...[/wobble]`, etc. Unescaped `[WASD]` is parsed as a tag; no `[/WASD]` → "unclosed tags" error.

## Convention
